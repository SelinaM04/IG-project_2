Food Scrap Drop-Off Locations

This website provides an interactive map and data visualizations to help New Yorkers easily find food scrap drop-off locations and make informed choices about sustainable waste disposal. Users can explore operating hours, understand the role of various site hosts, and compare resources across boroughs. Seasonal trends and accessibility insights are also highlighted, making it simple to engage with food scrap collection efforts year-round.

"Frequency of Operating Hours for Food Scrap Drop-Off Locations in NYC" provides an overview of the days on which food scrap drop-off sites are most frequently available.

Saturdays have the most operating drop-off sites, while Sundays and Fridays come in close second. Tuesday (and Monday as well) has the least amount of sites available, showing less accessibility at the start of the week. This pattern emphasizes the need for planning ahead by those looking to get in on food scrap recycling in particular on days with reduced availability.

The distribution of food scrap drop-off locations across New York City.

Manhattan has the highest number of sites indicating a higher demand or greater city planning efforts to manage food waste efficiently in dense urban areas. Staten Island has the least number of sites, which reflect less coverage or a smaller population needing such services.
